# Privacy Policy — Wormhole or Die

_Last updated: 25 June 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains what information the **Wormhole or Die** game for iPhone,
iPad, and Apple Vision Pro ("the App", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the
choices you have. We designed the App to collect as little as possible.

## Summary

- We collect **anonymous usage analytics** to understand how the game is played and
  to improve it.
- We do **not** collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, microphone, or
  camera.
- We do **not** track you across other apps or websites, and we do **not** use the
  advertising identifier (IDFA). The App shows no ads.
- On **Apple Vision Pro**, your look direction (head/device position) and pinch gestures
  control the game, processed **on your device**. This spatial data is never collected,
  stored, or sent to us.
- We do **not** sell your data.

## Information we collect

### Anonymous usage analytics
The App uses [PostHog](https://posthog.com) to collect anonymous product-interaction
analytics — for example: screens viewed, runs started and ended, scores, level
progress, and settings changes. These events are tied to a randomly generated
identifier created on your device. This identifier is **not** linked to your name,
email, Apple ID, or any other personal identity, and it is **not** used to track you
across other companies' apps or websites.

App version and build number are attached to events so we can tell which release a
report came from.

### Game Center
If you use leaderboards or multiplayer, those features are provided by Apple's **Game
Center**. Apple manages your Game Center account; we only receive your Game Center
display name and the scores you submit. Apple's handling of your data is governed by
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Spatial data on Apple Vision Pro (processed on-device only)
On Apple Vision Pro, you steer your ship with **pinch-and-drag** gestures and by looking
around — the App uses your **head/device position** to aim where you look. The system
provides this information to the App **only while you are playing**. It is used in the
moment to control gameplay and is **never** recorded, stored, collected, or transmitted
to us or anyone else. The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any hand, body, or
other spatial data, and does **not** access the device cameras or your surroundings.

### Data stored only on your device
Your settings, high scores, and preferences are saved locally on your device (in the
app's storage) and are not transmitted to us.

## How we use information

We use anonymous analytics solely to:

- Understand which features and levels are used,
- Find and fix bugs and performance problems,
- Improve game balance and the overall experience.

## How information is shared

We do not sell your data and do not share it for advertising. Anonymous analytics
events are processed on our behalf by PostHog, our analytics provider. Game Center
data is handled by Apple. We do not share data with anyone else.

## Your choices

You can turn analytics off at any time: open the App's **Settings** and disable the
analytics toggle. When disabled, the App stops sending analytics events.

## Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The analytics
we collect are anonymous and not directed at identifying any individual.

## Data retention

Anonymous analytics are retained by our analytics provider for as long as needed to
operate and improve the game, after which they are deleted or aggregated.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
